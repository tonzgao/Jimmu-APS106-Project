To make your own grid, please follow the following steps:
1. Write the size of your grid in the form 'length width' with spaces in between.
2. On a seperate line, write our your grid using the letters b, g, r, or y to represent points on the grid.
3. Save the file with a distinctive name in the Data folder.
4. When you play the game, select option 2 and provide the path to your grid.
